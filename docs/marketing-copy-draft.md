## 1. Landing Page Copy

### Hero

**Headline:**
Run your PG without the rent-day headache.

**Subheadline:**
NivaOps tracks every tenant, every bed, and every rupee — and chases rent for you with automatic payment links. Built for Indian PG owners who are done managing everything in their head, WhatsApp, and a half-finished Excel sheet.

### Feature Blurbs

**Stop chasing rent, start collecting it.**
When rent is due, NivaOps automatically sends your tenant a Razorpay payment link by SMS — and marks it paid the moment they pay. No "bhaiya, kal pakka" follow-ups, no manual tallying.

**Know exactly who owes what, at a glance.**
Green means paid, amber means due soon, red means overdue. One dashboard shows pending rent, occupancy %, and vacant beds — so you never lose track of a single tenant again.

**End deposit disputes before they start.**
Every security deposit is recorded on move-in and marked returned or forfeited on move-out. When a tenant asks "where's my deposit?", you have the answer in writing — not in your memory.

**Finally see if your PG is actually making money.**
Log expenses, track income, and get a clean P&L and cashflow view. No accountant, no spreadsheet formulas — just a clear picture of your business every month.

### Pricing

**Starter — ₹799/month**
For single-property owners. One property, up to 25 beds, everything included — rent tracking, auto payment links, deposits, expenses, and the full dashboard. Less than the cost of one late rent payment.

**Pro — ₹1,499/month**
For growing operators. Multiple properties, unlimited beds, all in one account. Manage your whole portfolio from one phone.

*Works right from your phone — install it like an app on Android or iPhone, no app store needed.*

## 3. Pitch / Positioning Narrative

I run a PG myself, and I built NivaOps because I was tired of rent day.

Every month it was the same: scrolling WhatsApp to see who paid, checking a notebook for deposits, mentally counting vacant beds. Money slipped through the cracks — not because tenants were bad, but because nobody was keeping proper track.

NivaOps fixes that. It's a simple app on your phone that knows every tenant, every bed, and every rupee. When rent is due, it automatically sends the tenant a payment link by SMS. When they pay, it's marked paid — automatically. No chasing, no tallying.

You also see everything in one place: who's overdue, which beds are empty, what your PG actually earned this month after expenses. Deposits are recorded properly, so move-out day has no arguments.

It's ₹799 a month — less than one day of one bed's rent. I use it every day for my own hostel. Give it a month, and rent day will stop being your most stressful day.
